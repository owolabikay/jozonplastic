About

Welcome to Jozon, your go-to destination for quality products in Nigeria. Our mission is to provide exceptional customer service and create a seamless shopping experience for you. Enjoy our loyalty programs and benefit from the expertise of our qualified staff. Shop with confidence at Jozon and discover a wide range of products tailored to your needs.

Mission

Our mission is to passionately innovate and manufacture environmentally conscious plastic products, candles, soaps, and more. We are dedicated to producing high-quality, biodegradable items that contribute to a sustainable future.

Vision

We envision a world where our factory is a global leader in the production of biodegradable plastic products, candles, and soaps. By prioritizing innovation and sustainability, we aim to enhance the well-being of humanity and leave a positive impact on the planet for generations to come.
